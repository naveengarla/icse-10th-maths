# 📦 Chapter 1 — GST

## What this chapter covers

Goods and Services Tax (GST) replaced VAT, Excise Duty and Service Tax in India from July 2017.
For the ICSE board exam, you need to handle **20 question types**. All are covered here.

## The golden rule

> **GST is always calculated on the Selling Price (after discount) — never on the Marked Price.**
