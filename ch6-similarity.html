📐 Chapter 6 — Similarity

Before we start — a promise​

If geometry has felt confusing since class 8, that's not your fault. It's because it was taught as rules to memorise instead of things to see. This chapter fixes that — every single concept has a diagram.

By the end of these pages you will be able to:

  • Look at two triangles and immediately say whether they are similar
  • Set up the right ratio and find unknown sides
  • Solve area and map problems confidently
